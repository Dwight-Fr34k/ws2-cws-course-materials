This collection of slides form the course materials for the Webscripting2 — Clientside Webscripting course, part of the Professional Bachelor ICT study programme, taught at Odisee, Ghent, Belgium.

The materials were developed by Rogier van der Linde, lecturer ICT at KaHo Sint-Lieven.

The materials may be used freely, as long as credit to Rogier van der Linde is present and the top right graphical link to ikdoeict.be remains in place.
